What is a MetaBeat?

The MetaBeat platform is built around NFT creations, participants, and rewards related to an artist's intellectual property (IP).

As NFT creations and users grow on the platform, partnerships between creators or artists and fans are strengthened through positive symbiotic experiences facilitated by the MetaBeat ecosystem that reflect the values ​​and contributions of users in the MetaBeat community.

Participants will have a special experience of being rewarded with Beat, a token of MetaBeat, through three business models: Drops, Mingle, and Shout-out, and further contribute to the revival of the entertainment industry.

What are Drops?

The NFTs are generated by diverse projects such as music albums, singles, metaverse concerts, fan meetings, etc.

Users purchase direct ownership of community NFTs through auctions at MetaBeat Drops and winners of the auction can enjoy rewards based on their FANomance Index generated from the ownership.

How can I join Drops?

You can participate in Drops by selecting a product on the MetaBeat app HOME > DROPS page and clicking the Open Run (participation) button.

After the end of Drops, among the number of participants who participated in the open run, the winning bid is decided by the lowest winning bid method, starting with the user who participated with the highest amount according to the amount of NFT issuance.

What if I want to sell my Drops?

You can sell the NFT you want in the NFT section of the MY BEAT > MY WALLET page of the MetaBeat app, and secondary transactions are possible through the sale and purchase list on the HOME > MARKETPLACE page.

In the case of drops, are there any other benefits other than owning NFTs when participating?

For NFT holders who have already participated in our Drops, we plan to offer pre-sale benefits related to the artist's NFT or pre-purchase limited edition NFT to be released later, and we will provide concert tickets or fan meeting tickets through consultation with the artist. The expected.

How to Safely trade and store Drop (NFT)?

Never do any trading other than the trading method not announced by MetaBeat.

Do not click on suspicious files or links received through SCR files or DMs.

Do not sign suspicious MetaMask transactions, and do not disclose your seed phrases to anyone other than yourself. Most DMs are likely to be scams. Always be careful.

What is Mingle?

Stake and get rewarded for artist activity!

Each Mingle is backed by real assets, music IP, and by staking BEAT on an open Mingle, you can receive rewards according to Mingle's FANomance Index.

Create your own portfolio with free and diverse Mingle participation.

How can I join Mingle?

You can participate in Mingle by selecting a product from the HOME > MINGLE page of the MetaBeat app and matching the available pieces (PCS).

Mingle who has completed participation can modify and cancel the pieces at any time.

What is a Shout Out?

Cheer on the artist on my social media and get rewards!

Shout-out is a participatory reward program that rewards users for promotional and marketing activities for their artists.

You can receive BEAT rewards according to performance by directly uploading posts about artist activities on MetaBeat or uploading them to various personal social media accounts and authenticating them to MetaBeat.

What is FANomance Index?

MetaBeat created the FANomance Index.

The FANomance Index is a customized index uniquely designed by MetaBeat, based on key criteria of artists and fan community performance.

Though simple, the index represents MetaBeat’s philosophy that lies in the shared distribution of a value system. The index criteria includes:

1. Commitment to Hold and Stake
2. Performance data gathered from social media buzz on artists
3. Performance data gathered from ShoutOut Fandom buzz on platform
