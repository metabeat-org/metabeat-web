The MetaBeat platform is an incentivization community platform powered by fan activities rewarded by a set criteria, the FANomance Index.

On MetaBeat, fans can support and grow with their favorite artists and music, while earning rewards.

Fans on MetaBeat build and strengthen partnerships with Creators/Artists through interactive and rewarding experiences fueled by the platform’s ecosystem.

With access to one of the largest K-pop music IP pools, fans on MetaBeat build and strengthen partnerships with Creators/Artists through interactive and rewarding experiences fueled by the platform’s ecosystem.

MetaBeat’s ecosystem converts fans’ social media activity into direct benefits by tokenizing their contributions on the platform.

The experience doesn’t stop there as fans are also rewarded for the music’s performance (such as airplay, streaming, etc.) and the Fandom’s performance within MetaBeat’s platform.
