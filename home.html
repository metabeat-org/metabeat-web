Solana-based
Fan 2 Earn

MetaBeat is a Solana-based, F2E (Fan2Earn), music IP NFT platform.

MetaBeat gives traditional music IP NFT auctions a unique twist with a fan-focused service.

With MetaBeat, the copyrights of the NFT are fully transferred to the purchaser via Neighboring Copyright.

These rights are protected by Korean copyright law, which ensures the purchaser’s rights are protected for 70 years from the date of publishing.
