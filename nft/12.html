ONEUS, the last page of the US series trilogy [FLY WITH US] released
You and I spread our wings to fly together.

“May the moon shine bright so that we can find you even in the dark”

RBW boy group ONEUS will release [FLY WITH US], which will decorate the end of the mini album 'US' series.

ONEUS is a six-member performance concept idol consisting of RAVN, Seoho, Leedo, Keonhee, Hwanwoong, and Xion, and has clearly stood out as a next-generation K-pop hot rookie representing 2019. In the meantime, ONEUS, whose members have directly participated in the entire album production process and showed the image of a complete idol, is back again with a high-quality album that will meet fans' expectations.

The last US series completed with this album is "Light of the Sun" from the 1st album 'LIGHT US', "Earth Hour" from the 2nd album 'RAISE US', and "Fly to the moon space" from the 3rd album [FLY WITH US] The series was completed with the theme of "the process of the light of the sun passing through the time of the earth and flying to the space of the moon". Just as a color is completed when light, time, and space meet, the light of the sun, the time of the earth, and the space of the moon meet to complete the color of ONEUS, and it contains the message that fans and the world will be painted with that color.

The title song 'LIT' is a song that expresses the way to fly together to the space of the moon as a splendid and enjoyable time under a bright moonlit night. It is shown. In particular, the cheerful melody and the lyrics that make use of the beauty of Hangeul are impressive.

The music video for "LIT" is directed by Hong Won-ki, director of Johnny Bros, who has worked with ONEUS every time, and shows a differentiated concept. The beautiful beauty of Korea is tightly captured in the video, and it is magnificent and adds visual richness, and it is full of scenes that maximize the performance of ONEUS, along with the various performances of the members.

In particular, ONEUS, which has been a hot topic every time with fresh performances, this time's title song 'LIT' also leaves a strong impression with its colorful and addictive performance, which contains Korean excitement.

The 3rd mini album [FLY WITH US] includes the title song 'LIT', 'Intro: Fly me to the moon', 'Plastic Flower', 'Blue Sky', 'Level Up', It is composed of a total of 6 b-side songs with the participation of top producers in Korea, including 'Stand By', and is musically diverse and rich.
