Mamamoo started the successful voyage of the 'Four Seasons Four Color Project' by sweeping various charts through the title song <Starry Night> of [Yellow Flower] in March of this year. The new mini-album [RED MOON], featuring Moonbyul's 'RED', was released for the second time in this project that captures the hidden charms and capabilities of each member that have not been shown before with each symbol in four colors. [RED MOON] is a combination of Mamamoo's second color, red, and Moon, meaning Moonbyul, and is expected to heat up the hot summer by pouring out Mamamoo's passionate charm that resembles summer.

This mini album contains a total of 6 songs. The title song of Moonbyul's solo album [SELFISH], including <Rainy Season>, which was released as a surprise release as a pre-release on the 8th and ranked first in the music chart for the first time in a girl group. is included. In addition, it is performed with a minor chord and makes one part of the heart cool with the horror atmosphere <Summer Night's Dream>, especially <From Today We>, <Running Through Time>, <You> of the girl group 'Girlfriend'. And I, Lee Gi and Yong-bae, who produced 'I', will participate as a new member and work on the song 'Sky Sky (Innocent)', showing Mamamoo's unique innocence that has been hidden in the past. The title song <You and You> is a collaboration between hit maker composer Kim Do-hoon, who is the general producer of Mamamoo's album, and Mamamoo's best friend, producer Park Woo-sang, making it possible to expect the birth of eight consecutive hit songs. This intense and hot song that evokes the image of a passionate summer is like a cider-like song that gives a hint to a lover who is selfish and takes care of himself first. In particular, as Mamamoo has gained the sympathy of women with music that represents the candid and dignified image of a woman, this new song <You Me> will once again show off her girl-crush charm. Mamamoo is showing each and every one of her charms that have deepened through the 'Four Seasons Four Color Project'.
