MAMAMOO released their 6th mini-album "Yellow Flower" in March and successfully finished their promotions. Amid active individual activities featuring hidden charms and capabilities, as predicted in a year when Mamamoo's music and talent bloom, Moon Byul's solo album release has also been reported for the first time in 4 years since her debut.

Moon Byul, who has shown endless musical development in recognition of her powerful rap skills as well as performance and sensible rap making through Mamamoo's activities, came to fans as a multi-musician by showing stable vocal skills through this album. For the first solo album [SELFISH] she worked with her best-friend producer Park Woo-sang, who worked outstandingly with Mamamoo, and Moon Byul also actively participated in making the album, including the writing of the lyrics and singing in the chorus.

With a beautiful voice from one of the best girl groups, Moonbyul, a talented rapper, and Seulgi, the SM tone fairy, their collaboration is something that is that is amazing to listen to.
