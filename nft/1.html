[White Wind] Marking of the end of Mamamoo's 'Four Seasons Four Color Project' has been release!

- Mamamoo's 'CHILL' in white party time!
- Four people, four colors, unique concept digestibility 'I'm going crazy'


Mamamoo, who announced the start of the 'Four Seasons Four Color Project' last spring and successfully opened the door with their first album [Yellow Flower], followed by [RED MOON] and [BLUE;S], receiving great love both in Korea and abroad. Mamamoogi, which drew trends and issues with unique performances and point choreography in each album, and made a big impact on various music sites and performing arts, always comes with the modifier 'trust and listen'.

It's March again, when the spring vibes are gently felt. Mamamoo came with [White Wind] to mark the end of the 'Four Seasons Four Color Project'.

[White Wind], the ninth mini album and the last album of the 'Four Seasons Four Color Project', was completed by adding 'white', the symbol color of Wheein, and 'wind' following the flowers, stars and sun, which are the symbols of each member. The album title [White Wind], which goes well with Wheein's clean and pure image, contains the history of Mamamoo's first meeting until now on a white canvas. In particular, the debut song in Mamamoo's group concept photo, which was released for the first time. The color and composition of the movie were reproduced as it is, giving fans memories.

[White Wind]'s title song <gogobebe> was composed by general producer Kim Do-hoon, who always creates new concepts for Mamamoo. A cheerful Mamamoo who is not obsessed with past love and events, and is enjoying it by using the lyrics and melody of the song 'Gigi Bebe Crying Birds. can meet

The members, who were tired of boring and boring daily life, receive an invitation to a house party and start preparing to go to the party with a pleasant imagination. Make shopping a lot, and make the dress code bling bling. However, the party venue that went into the wrong place due to the pranks of the local kids is a book club with an old-fashioned atmosphere! However, the embarrassment is short-lived, and Mamamoo turns even that place into a party room with their own energy.

When the first music video teaser was released, it was nicknamed 'Song of Fellowship' from fans, raising expectations. Mamamoo's unique concept digestibility is also expected to prove once again through <gogobebe>.

<Where RU>, a song about the thrill of waiting for a fateful lover like a hide-and-seek, and <Waggy>, which impresses with the humorous and witty lyrics of the bossa nova style, are full of charm like a lovely and cute spring breeze.
