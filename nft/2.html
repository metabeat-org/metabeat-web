Mamamoo's 6th mini album [Yellow Flower] release

Mamamoo is releasing a mini album [Yellow Flower] that will color this spring with their own brilliance.

The album name of the album [Yellow Flower] is a combination of the member Hwasa's color 'yellow' and 'flower', which means a flower named after Hwasa. As yellow symbolizes spring and a new start, it is an album where Mamamoo's music and talents, which are making their first start in 2018, blossom.

This mini album contains a total of 7 songs. In particular, in this album, Mamamoo's leader Solar's self-composed song 'Star Wind Flower Sun' is included as a sub-title song. Before the release, the song was released as a teaser video to express gratitude to the fans on Solar's birthday, drawing great attention.

In addition, the intro titled 'From Winter to Spring', the title song 'Starry Night', the solo song 'Becoming Dumb' with the candid and honest lyrics of Hwasa, the main character of this album's color, and the storytelling lyrics of fishery management are attractive. It contains 'Rude Boy', 'Bomta' with a sweet acoustic sound, and 'Paint Me', which was released as a single in January.
