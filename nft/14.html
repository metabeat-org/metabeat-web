ONEUS, depicting the time of dawn and sunset [RAISE US]
The second story of the 'US' series, 'The Sun is Falling'

RBW's rookie boy group ONEUS releases the second mini-album [RAISE US] of the 'US' series.

Consisting of RAVN, Seoho, Leedo, Keonhee, Hwanwoong, and Xion, ONEUS took their first steps in the music industry with their debut album 'LIGHT US' in January. Upon the release of their debut album, they topped the iTunes K-pop album charts in 4 countries, garnering attention from around the world and establishing themselves as next-generation K-pop prospects.

This mini-album [RAISE US] is an album with the meaning of “lifting us up.” If the last debut album, 'LIGHT US,'' meant “ONEUS, born with the light people shine on, the music they make,” , This album contains the meaning of “ONEUS, which will take a higher leap with the light that people shine on.”

In addition, [RAISE US] has the theme of the time before sunrise, when the darkness has not yet completely gone, and around sunset, just before it gets dark in earnest. It refers to countless times, and the motif was taken from a French proverb, 'the time between dogs and wolves'. It is an album with a concept of the contrasting duality as a keyword, with the theme of the red hour of sunset when the sun goes down and the blue hour of dawn when the sun rises.

The title song 'Twilight' is a song that expresses the concept of this album, 'the time between dogs and wolves (when the sun goes down)', and top producer Kim Do-hoon participated in the composition. A more complete song was created by adding impressive lyrics that compared the falling sun to a lover breaking up along with a delicate yet intense sound.

The music video for 'Twilight' was directed by Johnny Bros Hong Won-ki, based on the album's concept and the song's lyrics, and was all filmed in Italy. It created a visual beauty. The time of waiting and longing for a lover who left her side at dawn, and the time of regret at the moment the sun goes dark were captured in a vague yet intense video.

In particular, world-renowned choreographer 'Keone Madrid' participated in the choreography of the title song, further maximizing the performance of 'Twilight'.

In addition, the [RAISE US] album includes the title song 'Twilight', 'Intro: Time', 'English Girl', 'Time Between Dog and Wolf (BingBing)', 'White Night' (White Night) It is expected that this album will also show off the colorful charms of ONEUS, with a total of 6 b-side songs including 'Night' and 'Now'.
