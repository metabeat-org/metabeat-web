Feature

Mamamoo's new song "Paint Me", released for the first time in the new year, song written by 5 producers including Peter Wallevik, who worked with famous foreign artists such as Little Mix, Olly Murs, Kylie Minogue, One Direction, and Demi Lovato. Despite the restrained session composition with picturesque lyrics that metaphorically express the excitement and warmth of love, the cold pain of parting, and the passionate emotions that become hot again in four colors: white, yellow, blue, and red. The rich ambience, the melody of the main piano that holds the center of the song, the guitar sound that continues to give tension to the song, and the strings in the second half add to the music of a grand scale reminiscent of a blockbuster movie OST. In addition, vocals that express the emotions of each member in their own way were added to increase the concentration of the song. In particular, 'Moon Byul', well known for her rap position in 'Mamamoo', through this song, transformed into a vocalist and sang with a mournful voice, boldly showing off her hidden vocal skills, exuding charm.
