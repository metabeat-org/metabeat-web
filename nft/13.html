Super rookie's prelude Rookie boy group 'ONEUS' 1st Mini Album [LIGHT US] released!

RBW's first boy group ONEUS' debut album [LIGHT US] will be released on January 9th.
ONEUS, which consists of 6 members, RAVN, Seoho, Leedo, Keonhee, Hwanwoong, and Xion, has the meaning of 'we (US) made by gathering the power of each fan (ONE)'. It also contains the meaning of 'in the end, ordinary people come together to create one world'.

ONEUS, ambitiously prepared by RBW, who has led the success of talented musicians, is an artist-type performance group that not only looks good, but also has the ability to write lyrics, compose, produce, and create choreography. Fans have already been watching active activities such as numerous performances, entertainment and busking through the project called 'I will debut' for about 1 year and 10 months.
